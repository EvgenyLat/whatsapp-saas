We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Principle of least privilege
- Regular access reviews

**Infrastructure Security:**
- Firewalls and intrusion detection
- Network segmentation
- DDoS protection
- Regular security patches

**Application Security:**
- Input validation and sanitization
- SQL injection prevention
- XSS protection
- CSRF protection
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

**Monitoring:**
- 24/7 security monitoring
- Automated threat detection
- Incident response procedures
- Security event logging

### 5.2 Data Isolation

- Each customer's data is logically isolated

- Security training and awareness programs
- Non-disclosure agreements

### 5.4 Vendor Security

- All vendors undergo security assessments
- Vendors must meet our security standards
- Regular vendor audits
- Vendor contracts include security requirements

### 5.5 Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:
- Keeping your password secure
- Enabling two-factor authentication
- Monitoring your account for suspicious activity
- Reporting security incidents promptly
- Securing your own devices and networks

### 5.6 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:
- We will investigate promptly
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours

- We will provide details about the breach and our response

### 5.7 Limitations

While we strive to protect your information, no security measures are 100% effective. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service.

### 6.2 After Account Closure

When you close your account:
- We retain data for 30 days for account recovery
- After 30 days, we delete or anonymize your data
- Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes

### 6.3 Specific Retention Periods

**Account Information:**
- Retained while account is active
- Deleted 30 days after account closure

**Payment Information:**
- Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
- Credit card numbers are tokenized and stored by payment processor

**Messages and Conversations:**
- Retained per your retention settings (default: 90 days)
- You can configure retention period in settings
- Can be deleted immediately upon request

**Support Tickets:**
- Retained for 3 years for quality assurance and training

**Logs and Analytics:**
- Aggregated logs: 2 years
- Individual logs: 90 days
- Security logs: 1 year

**Legal Holds:**
- Data subject to legal holds is retained until the hold is lifted

### 6.4 Deletion Requests

You can request deletion at any time:
- Through your account settings
- By contacting support
- We will complete deletion within 30 days
- Some data may be retained as legally required

## 12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 12.1 Types of Cookies

We use the following cookies:

**Strictly Necessary Cookies:**
- Authentication and session management
- Security features
- Load balancing
- Cannot be disabled

**Functional Cookies:**
- Remember your preferences
- Language settings
- Display settings
- Can be disabled in settings

**Analytics Cookies:**
- Google Analytics
- Usage statistics
- Performance monitoring
- Can be disabled (see opt-out below)

**Marketing Cookies:**
- We do not use marketing cookies
- Third-party marketing cookies are blocked

### 12.2 Cookie Duration

- **Session cookies**: Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies**: Stored for a specified period (typically 1-2 years)

### 12.3 Cookie Management

**Browser Settings:**
- Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies
- You can delete existing cookies
- See your browser's help documentation

**Opt-Out Links:**
- Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

**Impact of Disabling:**
- Some features may not work properly
- You may need to re-enter preferences

### 12.4 Other Tracking Technologies

**Web Beacons/Pixels:**
- Used in emails to track opens
- Used to measure email effectiveness
- Can be blocked by image blocking in email client

**Local Storage:**
- Stores preferences locally
- Similar to cookies but with larger capacity
- Can be cleared through browser settings

### 12.5 Third-Party Tracking

We do not allow third-party advertising networks to track users on our Service.

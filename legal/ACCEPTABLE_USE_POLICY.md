## 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of the WhatsApp SaaS Platform ("Service"). This AUP is part of our Terms of Service and applies to all users.

**By using the Service, you agree to comply with this AUP. Violation may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account without refund.**

## 2. Acceptable Use

You may use the Service only for lawful purposes and in accordance with:
- This Acceptable Use Policy
- Our Terms of Service
- WhatsApp Business Policy
- WhatsApp Commerce Policy
- All applicable laws and regulations

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### 3.1 Illegal Activities

You may NOT use the Service to:
- Engage in any illegal activity
- Violate any local, state, national, or international law
- Promote or facilitate illegal goods, services, or activities
- Infringe on intellectual property rights
- Engage in fraud, identity theft, or deception
- Launder money or finance terrorism
- Violate export control or economic sanctions laws

### 3.2 Spam and Unsolicited Messages

You may NOT:
- Send unsolicited bulk messages (spam)
- Send messages to recipients who have not opted in
- Use purchased, rented, or harvested contact lists
- Send messages after recipient has opted out
- Fail to honor opt-out requests
- Send excessive volumes of messages
- Use deceptive subject lines or sender information
- Send messages that violate CAN-SPAM Act, TCPA, or similar laws

### 3.3 Harassment and Abuse

You may NOT:
- Harass, threaten, or intimidate others
- Send abusive or hateful content
- Stalk or repeatedly contact unwilling recipients
- Impersonate others or misrepresent your identity
- Collect personal information without consent
- Violate the privacy rights of others

### 3.4 Harmful or Malicious Content

You may NOT transmit:
- Viruses, malware, or other harmful code
- Phishing attempts or social engineering attacks
- Content designed to exploit security vulnerabilities
- Automated scripts or bots without authorization
- Content that damages, disables, or impairs the Service

### 3.5 Inappropriate Content

You may NOT send content that is:
- Defamatory, libelous, or slanderous
- Obscene, pornographic, or sexually explicit
- Violent, graphic, or gratuitously offensive
- Promoting hatred, discrimination, or violence
- Involving minors in harmful or exploitative ways
- Infringing on others' rights of publicity or privacy

### 3.6 Prohibited Industries and Activities

You may NOT use the Service for:
- **Illegal Substances**: Drugs, narcotics, controlled substances
- **Weapons**: Firearms, explosives, ammunition (except licensed dealers)
- **Gambling**: Unlicensed gambling or betting services
- **Adult Content**: Pornography or adult services
- **Tobacco**: E-cigarettes, vaping products (in restricted jurisdictions)
- **Multi-Level Marketing**: Pyramid schemes or chain letters
- **Counterfeit Goods**: Fake or unauthorized replicas
- **Hacking Services**: Unauthorized access, cracking, or exploitation tools
- **Academic Dishonesty**: Essay mills, exam cheating services
- **Get-Rich-Quick Schemes**: Unrealistic financial promises

### 3.7 WhatsApp Policy Violations

You must comply with:
- WhatsApp Business Policy
- WhatsApp Commerce Policy
- WhatsApp Brand Guidelines
- Meta Community Standards

Prohibited activities include:
- Sending promotional content without proper opt-in
- Using unapproved message templates
- Failing to respond to customer inquiries within 24 hours
- Misleading users about business identity
- Selling prohibited goods or services
- Violating intellectual property in business profile

### 3.8 System Abuse

You may NOT:
- Circumvent rate limits or usage restrictions
- Reverse engineer or decompile the Service
- Access or use non-public areas of the Service
- Probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities
- Forge headers or identifiers
- Interfere with other users' access
- Use automated systems to access the Service (except via API)
- Create multiple accounts to evade restrictions
- Share account credentials with unauthorized users

### 3.9 Data Misuse

You may NOT:
- Scrape, harvest, or collect user data without authorization
- Use data for purposes other than intended
- Share user data with unauthorized third parties
- Sell or license user data
- Use data to build competing products
- Retain data longer than necessary

### 3.10 Payment and Financial Fraud

You may NOT:
- Use stolen payment methods
- Engage in chargeback fraud
- Provide false billing information
- Evade payment obligations
- Engage in credit card testing or carding

## 4. Responsible Use Guidelines

### 4.1 Message Content

- Be truthful and transparent
- Clearly identify your business
- Honor opt-out requests immediately
- Provide contact information
- Respect recipients' time and preferences
- Send relevant and valuable content

### 4.2 Data Handling

- Collect only necessary data
- Obtain proper consent
- Protect user privacy
- Comply with data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Implement appropriate security measures
- Delete data when no longer needed

### 4.3 API Usage

- Follow API documentation and best practices
- Implement proper error handling
- Respect rate limits
- Cache responses appropriately
- Use webhooks for real-time updates
- Monitor API usage and errors

### 4.4 Account Security

- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Do not share account credentials
- Monitor account activity
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Keep contact information current

We reserve the right to:
- Monitor usage of the Service

- Investigate suspicious activity
- Access accounts to ensure compliance
- Analyze usage patterns and trends

### 6.2 Reporting Violations

To report violations:
- Email: abuse@example.com
- Subject: "AUP Violation Report"
- Include: Account details, evidence, description

We investigate all reports promptly and confidentially.

### 6.3 Consequences of Violations

Violations may result in:

**First Offense:**
- Warning notice
- Temporary suspension (24-72 hours)
- Removal of violating content
- Requirement to acknowledge AUP

**Repeat Offenses:**
- Extended suspension
- Account termination
- No refund of fees
- Referral to law enforcement (if illegal activity)

**Immediate Termination:**
- Illegal activities
- Severe violations (spam, malware, fraud)
- Posing risk to other users or the Service
- Violating court orders or injunctions

### 6.4 Appeals Process

If your account is suspended or terminated:
1. Email: appeals@example.com within 14 days
2. Include: Account details, explanation, corrective actions
3. We will review within 7 business days
4. Final decision is at our sole discretion

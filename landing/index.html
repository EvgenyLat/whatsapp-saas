What it does
Everything your salon
needs — automated

AI Intent Classification

Understands what clients want — booking, cancellation, rescheduling — in Hebrew, Russian, and English. No rigid menus.

📅

Smart Scheduling

Real-time availability checking, automatic conflict prevention, and reminder messages before each appointment.

How it works
From message to
confirmed booking
01

Client sends a WhatsApp message

Any natural language — "I want a haircut tomorrow" or "Can I cancel my 3pm?" — in any supported language.

02

AI classifies intent instantly

The NestJS backend routes the message through the AI layer, identifies intent, extracts date/time/service preferences.

03

System checks availability & confirms

PostgreSQL stores appointments, Redis handles session state. Conflicts are prevented automatically.

04

Reminder sent automatically

Scheduled reminder messages reduce no-shows. Salon owner sees everything in the admin dashboard.

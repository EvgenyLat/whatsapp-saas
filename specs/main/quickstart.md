   - **Email**: For notifications
   - **Working Hours**: Set their schedule for each day

3. Click **"Save"**
4. Success! Your first staff member is added âœ…

### Set Up Working Hours

Working hours determine when customers can book with this staff member.

**Example:**
- **Monday-Friday**: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- **Saturday**: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- **Sunday**: Off (leave empty)

**Tips:**
- Leave a day empty if they don't work that day
- Working hours are shown in 24-hour format (9:00 = 9 AM, 18:00 = 6 PM)
- Set breaks by splitting shifts (e.g., 9-13, 14-18 for lunch break)

### Add More Staff

Repeat the process for each team member. Most salons have 3-10 staff members.

### Deactivating (Not Deleting)

**Why Deactivate Instead of Delete?**
- Past bookings remain intact
- History is preserved
- Can reactivate anytime

**To Deactivate:**
1. Open staff/service details
2. Toggle "Active" switch to Off
3. Confirm
4. Item won't appear in new bookings but history remains

## ğŸ†˜ Common Questions

**Q: Can I add staff later?**
A: Yes! Add staff anytime. Existing bookings won't be affected.

**Q: What happens if I change a service price?**
A: New price applies to future bookings only. Past bookings keep original price.

**Q: Can staff work different hours each day?**
A: Yes! Set custom hours for each day of the week.

**Q: How do I handle staff vacations?**
A: Temporarily mark them as inactive, then reactivate when they return.

**Q: Can one service have multiple prices?**
A: Create separate service entries (e.g., "Women's Haircut - Short" and "Women's Haircut - Long").

**Q: What if I delete something by mistake?**
A: Contact support immediately. Deactivated items can be reactivated, but deleted items may not be recoverable.

**Q: How many specializations can one staff member have?**
A: Unlimited! Select all that apply.

**Q: Can I import staff/services from a spreadsheet?**
A: Not in the current version. Future update will support bulk import.
